Explore Our Vision

Harmonizing Structure & Nature

Rooted in the landscapes of Michigan, designing for the world.

At Grand View Architectural Designs LLC, we believe that true architecture does not impose itself upon the land, but rather emerges from it. Founded by Billie Leveque, our firm is dedicated to the principle of "specialized design services" that prioritize site-specificity and environmental context.

Located in Shingleton, we draw inspiration from the diverse topography of the region to create structures that are both resilient and aesthetically profound. Our team is composed of technical experts and creative visionaries who understand the unique challenges of building in variable climates.

We move beyond standard blueprints to offer a holistic design philosophy. From the initial concept sketch to the final structural detail, every line we draw is intentional. Our goal is to craft spaces that offer not just shelter, but a "Grand View" of the life you wish to lead.

Our Pathway to Completion

A structured approach to bringing complex ideas to life.

01.

Consultation & Feasibility

We begin with a deep dive into your goals and the physical constraints of the site. We conduct zoning checks and initial budget assessments to establish a realistic roadmap for the project.

02.

Design & Refinement

This is where creativity takes form. We produce sketches, 3D models, and detailed floor plans. We iterate on these designs based on your feedback until the concept is flawless.

03.

Documentation & Oversight

We finalize the technical blueprints for contractors and submit for permits. During construction, we provide architectural observation to ensure the build adheres to the specifications.
